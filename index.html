Healthy Diet

Eat smart, live strong, and stay healthy every day

What is a Healthy Diet?

A healthy diet provides your body with essential nutrients required for energy, growth, and disease prevention. It includes a balance of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water.

Balanced Diet Components

Vegetables

Rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. Eat a variety daily.

Fruits

Provide natural sugars, vitamins, and hydration.

Whole Grains

Provide long-lasting energy and support digestion.
